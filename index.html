Questions You're Probably Thinking

Before you email us

Is this legit? How do I know you won't disappear with my money?

Fair question. We work from Nairobi and plan each trip with you directly before anything is booked. You'll receive a written itinerary, clear pricing, and transparent payment steps before you decide.

How much does a safari cost?

Most trips run $3,000 to $8,000 per person depending on duration, lodges, and group size. We'll give you a detailed quote with no hidden fees after we talk.

What if I need to cancel?

Full refund up to 60 days before departure. 50% refund 30 to 60 days out. We also recommend travel insurance and can help you find the right policy.

Do you handle flights and visas?

Our team handles everything on the ground in Kenya. For flights, we'll recommend routes and timing. For visas, we'll walk you through the eVisa process... it takes about 10 minutes.

Is Kenya safe?

Our safari areas (Masai Mara, Amboseli, Samburu, Laikipia) are safe and well-organized. You'll be with experienced guides throughout, and we only recommend routes where we know the people and logistics well.
